Selfless Servant

As an Eagle Scout, I have grown up with a love for serving others. I have committed much of my life to helping others in whatever way I can, whether that involves volunteering, or working in a service organization. I truly believe that the most fufilling thing that you can do with your time is to give back to others.

Volunteering and Service Experience

Eagle Scout Project

One of the biggest service projects that I have completed was when I completed my Eagle Scout Project. This was a great experience where I led a group of scouts in clearing out a large area of overgrowth at the church my troop met at. We brought it all the way down to useable dirt, and tilled and added sod as well. This was a hugely rewarding experience

Fish Camp at Texas A&M

Something that I am very acive in now is Fish Camp at Texas A&M. This is a 3 day 2 night introduction to the Aggie family and traditions. I specifically am a chair for crew, who are the people helping run the behind the scenes. The entire identity of what we do revolves around the selfless service for the incoming freshmen.

Meals on Wheels

One of the first service experiences I had, which is what really got me passionate about it was the meals on wheels program. I started this when I was a young kid with my grandmother, and fell in love with helping others. We were able to deliver food to people who have difficulty getting it themselves, which can really make a difference in their day and even lives.
